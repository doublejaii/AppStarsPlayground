Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AppStarsPlayground ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our iOS score tracking application.

AppStarsPlayground is intended to help users track scores, sessions, and related game activity. We aim to limit data collection to what is reasonably necessary to operate the App.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Information you enter: player names, scores, match records, notes, and related game details.
  • Device and diagnostic information: app version, device type, operating system version, and crash information made available through Apple or platform tooling.
  • Support communications: information you provide if you contact us with a question, bug report, or feedback request.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide the App's core score tracking and history features.
  • Store, display, and manage game records that you choose to keep in the App.
  • Maintain app reliability, investigate crashes, and improve performance.
  • Respond to support requests and user feedback.

3. Data Storage

Information you enter into the App may be stored locally on your device. If the App uses Apple features such as device backup or iCloud synchronization, some information may also be stored or synchronized through services connected to your Apple account.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary:

  • With service providers that support hosting, diagnostics, or app operations, if those services are used.
  • To comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the App, or others.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the App, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Data stored on your device may remain until you delete it, clear the App's data, or remove the App.

6. Your Choices

  • You can edit or remove score records within the App if those features are available.
  • You can uninstall the App to remove locally stored data from your device, subject to any active backups.
  • You can manage iCloud and backup settings through your Apple device settings if those features apply.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and address the issue.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards intended to protect information. However, no storage or transmission method is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will revise the effective date on this page and may provide additional notice within the App when appropriate.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through our support form at https://forms.gle/epMLMbw1butR8CCTA.
